Flavin Neuromachines Lab · Georgia Tech

Augmenting how we sense

The Flavin Neuromachines Lab is conducting a study on wearable devices that augment a person's ability to sense obstacles in their surroundings. We're recruiting volunteers through the Georgia Tech Center for Inclusive Design and Innovation, the Georgia Tech Home Lab, the Georgia Center for the Visually Impaired, and the Georgia Institute of Technology campus.
